Regent Institute of Science and Technology

Regent Institute of Science and Technology, Kolkata (RIST Kolkata), was established in 2009 under the aegis of the Regent Group of Institutes.

The mission of RIST Kolkata is to improve students' academic knowledge, applied learning, and performance skills to ensure rewarding personal lives and careers while contributing to the community and economic progress in our diverse and evolving democratic society.

Approved by the All India Council of Technical Education (AICTE), RIST Kolkata is affiliated to the Maulana Abul Kalam Azad University of Technology (MAKAUT).
